Kraft Mac and Cheese

Whether you're making dinner for the family or needing a quick snack for yourself, Kraft Mac and Cheese will always make its consumer(s) happy.

This recipe is a simple and foolproof way to prepare Kraft Mac and Cheese so that everyone eating can love it.

Ingredients

  • Box of Kraft Mac and Cheese!
  • 6 Cups of Water
  • 1/4 Cup Butter
  • 1/4 Cup Milk

Steps

  1. Bring water to a boil in a medium saucepan.
  2. Reduce heat and add macaroni to water. Set the cheese packet aside.
  3. Cook for 7-7.5 minutes. Stir occassionally.
  4. Drain, but do not rinse the mac.
  5. Return the macaroni to the pan.
  6. Add milk, butter, and cheese packet. Mix well.
  7. Serve!
