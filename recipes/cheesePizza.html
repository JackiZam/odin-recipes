Simple Cheese Pizza

Who doesn't LOVE pizza? I know I do! With this simple recipe for a cheese pizza, you'll learn how you can make one of the world's favorite foods yourself! If you want any other toppings, you can use this recipe as your base-pizza-model!

This recipe has everything you need to know for making a cheese pizza. It talks about baking with yeast, pizza dough ingredients, necessary ingredients, and how to both make the dough and bake the pizza!

Ingredients

  • Premade pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Sliced Mozzarella Cheese
  • Shredded Mozzarella Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese/li>
  • Ground Basil or Italian Seasoning

Steps

  1. Bring dough to room temperature
  2. Preheat oven to 475 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Spread the dough so you have a circular shape. Knead it and toss it as neccessary.
  4. Top the dough with your desired amount of pizza sauce, then the slided mozzarella, then the shredded mozzarella, then the parmesan.
  5. Sprinkle basil on the sauce and cheese layers as desired.
  6. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
  7. Slice the pizza and serve it hot!
