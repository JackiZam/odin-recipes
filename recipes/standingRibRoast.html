Standing Rib Roast

Standing rib roast is considered by most to be the best roast out there. Certainly, it is my top choice when my grandma cooks. It has great flavor, texture, and juiciness, and it is big enough to serve many.

Of couse, it is worthy to note that the roast is not a small cut of meat. It's large size and popularity make it expensive, but that makes the roast even better on those rare special occasions that it is served! This recipe will discuss how to choose the right meat, the necessary ingredients, how to make a standing rib roast, and proper internal temperatures.

Ingredients

  • Rosemary
  • Thyme
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • The cut of meat!

And of course, you can add any other spices or herbs that you would like!

Steps

  1. Bring beef to room temperature
  2. Preheat oven to 460 degrees Fahrenheit
  3. Slather the beef in butter and onion
  4. Roast the beef for 20 minutes
  5. Remove the beef and slather the remaining butter on the beef.
  6. Turn the oven down to 250 degrees Fahrenheit
  7. Slow roast the beef for 1.5 more hours, basting it every 30 minutes with the juices in the pan. Do this until the internal temperature of the meat is 129 degrees Fahrenheit.
  8. Transfer the beef to a plate and let it rest for 20 - 30 minutes.
